1. Fill the five-quart jug from the supply.
2. Fill the three-quart jug with the water from five-quart jug.
3. The five-quart jug now has two quarts of water left.
4. Throw away the water from the three-quart jug and fill it with
   water from the five-quart jug. The five-quart jug is now empty
   and the three-quart jug has two quarts of water.
5. Fill the five-quart jug from the supply.
6. Fill the three-quart jug with the water from five-quart jug.
7. The five-quart jug now has four quarts of water.
